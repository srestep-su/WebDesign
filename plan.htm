A Note on Accessibility:

Accessible parking and restroom facilities are available. At least 60% of our exhibits are located indoors. Both the inside and outdoor pathways at the zoo are accessible. Wheelchairs are available free of charge on a first come, first served basis.

For more detailed information on accessibility at the Rosamond Gifford Zoo, visit the New York State Inclusive Recreation Resource Center page for our zoo.

Some insider tips for your upcoming visit:
  • Plan to spend at least two hours at the Zoo.
  • Check out the Zoo daily schedule upon arrival.
  • Interact with Zoo staff and use #BestDayEver on social media posts.
  • Before you leave the Zoo, be sure to stop in and check out all the amazing items they have to offer.
  • Pets are not permitted on zoo grounds, in the parking lot or in cars.
  • The Rosamond Gifford Zoo is a tobacco-free, smoke-free and vape-free facility.
  • We welcome you to take photographs and video of your experience at the zoo for personal use.
  • Have plenty of fun!
